Frequently Asked Question

What is the difference between Padding and Margin?

Padding is the space between the content and the border of an element, while Margin is the space outside the element’s border, separating it from other elements.

What is the use of z-index in CSS?

z-index controls the vertical stacking order of elements. Elements with a higher z-index value appear above those with lower values.

What does box-sizing: border-box; do?

box-sizing: border-box; includes padding and border in the element’s total width and height, preventing the element from becoming larger than its set dimensions.

What is the purpose of flexbox in CSS?

Flexbox is a layout module that makes it easy to design flexible and responsive layouts. It allows items to align, justify, and distribute space efficiently within a container.

What are media queries in CSS?

Media queries allow you to apply CSS styles based on device characteristics, such as screen width, height, or orientation, to make designs responsive.
